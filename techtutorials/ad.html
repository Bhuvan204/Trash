Advanced Keyboard Shortcuts


1.Left Alt + left Shift + Print Screen to turn High Contrast on or off.

2.Shift Key five times to turn Sticky Keys on or off.

3.Windows logo + '+' or '-' key to zoom in and out.

4.Shift + Right click to get full information of menu.

5.Ctrl+W to close window.

6.Windows +_R to open run program dialog box.

7.Windows + Shift + S to open snipping tool in windows 8 or later.

8.PrintScreen to take screenshots in windows 8 or higher.
