How switch between different languages in your PC


1.On your desktop go to toolbar.

2.Right click on blank space in toolbar.

3.Then go on toolbar option in the menu.

4.Then select the language bar from there.

5.Then you will se EN or you default language logo in toolbar.

6.Then you can easily switch between languages.

7.You can also download your languages to use.

8.Only downloaded languages will be there.
